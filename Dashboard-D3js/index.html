From the data Analysis,It can be clearly seen that:

  1. Births Were drastically decreasing between 2008-2014. After careful research it has be concluded the following were the reasons:
    • Employement of Birth Control
    • Economic Constrains
    • Marriage At late Age
    • Government Policies
  2. Births were increasing systematically between 2000 and 2014,The research conducted on the reason behind the growth are:
    • Birth Control was believed to cause diseases like cancer
    • The Economy by that time was favourable.
    • Early pregnacies were highly registered
    • The goverment had promised to give support after a certain number of children
